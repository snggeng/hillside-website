Sideline Superheroes

By Harry Liang

The student volunteers with the Hillside High School Athletic Training program are an integral part of the football program, making sure that the teams are set up for success and adding to the exciting environment of Friday Night Football.

For senior Kamri, the Athletic Training program is also a bridge to her future career goals.
